our problem:


  • Almost 12% of Americans suffer from food insecurity but up to 40% of food is wasted every year.
  • 25% of the world's fresh water goes to food that is wasted in landfills across the country, where the food decomposes and produces methane.
  • Food is wasted at all stages of production, but most markedly so at the consumer level, which accounts for almost half of all food waste.
  • This is in some part due to misleading food labels, which cause edible food to be discarded because of "early" expiration dates.

Throwing away our food is like throwing away our future.
